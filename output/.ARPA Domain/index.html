Domains Protocols Numbers About .ARPA Zone Management The .arpa domain is the “Address and Routing Parameter Area” domain and is designated to be used exclusively for Internet-infrastructure purposes. We administer the domain in cooperation with the Internet technical community through the guidance of the Internet Architecture Board . For the management guidelines and operational requirements of the .arpa domain, see RFC 3172 .
